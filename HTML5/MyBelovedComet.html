My Beloved Comet Hoshimachi Suisei

Hoshimachi Suisei (星街すいせい) is a female Japanese Virtual YouTuber associated with hololive. Originally an independent VTuber who eventually was recruited into hololive production's label INoNaKa Music, she was transferred to the main hololive branch on 1 December 2019. She is currently part of "hololive 0th gen" alongside fellow solo debutants: Tokino Sora, Roboco, Sakura Miko and AZKi.

Personality

Suisei is a modest, multi-talented singer and aspiring idol who is among the more level-headed members of hololive, though she also has a childish streak. After her unexpectedly compelling performance as a traitor in a game of Project Winter, she gained a reputation for remorseless sociopathy behind a facade of polite sweetness. No one seems to know how much of the "Psychopath Suisei" persona is real or not, but Suisei herself acknowledges that it is extremely funny and has referred to the incident on multiple occasions.

Appearance

She has light blue hair in a side ponytail and blue eyes. She wears a dark striped blue hair ribbon with a black plaid cap with a crown attached to it. She wears a grey plaid dress uniform with part of the skirt being dark blue and ruffly. She has black shoes and asymmetrical socks.

Career Highlights

  • Debuted her second 2D costume on 21 June.
  • Joined VTuber group S:gnal on 1 July.
  • Graduated from S:gnal and resumed activities as an independent VTuber on 4 August.
  • Debuted her third 2D costume on 11 January.
  • Joined INoNaKa Music on 19 May.
  • Transferred to hololive's main branch on 1 December.
  • Reached 1,000,000 YouTube subscribers on 26 June.
  • Released her first album "Still Still Stellar" on 29 September.
  • Reached 2,000,000 YouTube subscribers on 27 September.

Personal Life

Suisei lives with her older sister, Anemachi, who is very popular among Suisei's friends, especially Miko. She is notably good at playing competitive Tetris.

Fanbase

Her fanbase is officially called "Stargazers," while in Japanese they are called "Hoshiyomi" 「星詠み」.
